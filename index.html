How to make chocolate chip cookies




Introduction
This is a chocolate chip cookie recipe to make some delicious cookies. make sure you follow the instructions step by step for some fantastic results. Give yourself a lot of room in your kitchen and keep organized so you don't have much to clean.

Ingredients you will need:
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 12 ounces of chocolate chips
  • 2 1/4 cups of plain flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda

Instructions:
  1. Preheat the over to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Mix and sift all the dry ingredients
    • Pour flour, salt, an baking powder into a bowl and make sure to whisk them together.
  3. Cream the butter and sugars into a separate bowl.
    • Place them in a large bowl and use a hand mixer to beat them together intil the butter and sugars are combined into the mixture. It should turn to be light and fluffy.
